How About a Nice Game of Chess?

How Long Did That Take?

  • 48 Chess Boards
  • Average Game = 40 Moves
  • Suppose 1 Minute per Move
  • Synchronously = 40 Moves x 1 Minute x 48 Games = 1,920 Minutes = 32 Hours
